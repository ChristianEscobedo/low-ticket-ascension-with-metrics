# Millionaire Mindshift - VSL Scripts & Scene Plan

Mechanism-forward VSLs for the full funnel. Built off the live page copy so the video and the page tell the same story. Written for AI b-roll: every beat has a spoken line and a scene you can drop straight into Runway, Kling, Veo, or Sora.

---

## 0. How to use this doc

Each VSL is written in chapters, not sections. Every chapter gives you four things:

- **VO:** the exact words spoken. This is the script. Read it slow.
- **SCENE:** the AI b-roll prompt for that beat. Cinematic, black-and-gold, premium.
- **TEXT:** the on-screen words (kinetic captions / lower thirds).
- **WHY:** the puzzle pieces and brain chemistry firing, so you know what each beat is doing.

Timing math: VSL pacing is slow on purpose, about 130 words a minute. Main page lands near 3 minutes. The three upsell videos are short by design.

### Voice rules (locked)

No em dashes. No "not just X, but Y" patterns. No AI tells. Fifth grade words only, because the part of the brain we are talking to was wired by age seven. Short sentences. Then a long one when it earns it. First line of every VSL nails the positioning and the value prop. If it does not grab by the throat, it gets cut.

### AI b-roll style guide (paste into every prompt)

> Cinematic, shallow depth of field, 24fps film grain, slow deliberate camera moves, premium and moody. Color palette: deep black, warm gold, soft amber light. Volumetric light, subtle dust in the air, high contrast. No text in frame. Aspect 16:9 (also render 9:16 for ads).

Keep a person archetype consistent across shots: a 30s to 40s founder, well dressed, tired but sharp. Christian and Amber appear only in the founder beats (use real footage there if you have it).

### Hidden Enemy
The subconscious belief OS. Older than the strategy, louder than the strategy, last vote every time. It is not the offer, the funnel, or the niche. It is the operator running all three.

### Symbols and dog whistles
The ceiling that "moves up to meet you." The operating system underneath. Two belief sets voting in parallel (what you say vs what your body votes). The financial thermostat. The block under the block. The switch. "Why is this so hard for me" turning into "why didn't I do this years ago."
